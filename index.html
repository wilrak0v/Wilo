The Wilo Project is a collection of programs for free and open computing.

We follow a consistent philosophy across all our programs:

  • People are in control of their data.
  • The code is easy to read and understand.
  • The code is minimal by design.
  • We avoid using GAFAM products.
  • Our programs follow the UNIX philosophy: do one thing, and do it well.

We are currently working on a personal AI, an Android app store, a social network client, a Linux system, and several other projects.
